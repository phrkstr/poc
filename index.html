iOS Vulnerability PoC

Vulnerability: On iOS, while auto-downloads are rare, phishing attacks can trick you into entering sensitive data on fake login pages.

How It Works:

A phishing site might display a fake login prompt to steal credentials.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Verify URLs before clicking them.
  • Avoid downloading files from untrusted sources.
  • Keep your operating system and software updated.
  • Be wary of unsolicited prompts or updates.
